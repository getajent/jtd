Project Overview

This case study project for Aalborg Kommune involved creating a poster in both an animated digital format and a printed A3 version. The goal was to convey a warm "Merry Christmas" message to internationals living in or moving to the region, fostering a welcoming atmosphere to encourage them to continue living in Nordjylland.
