Project Details

Aalborg Kommune project focused on creating a comprehensive design system and marketing materials for the municipality. The goal was to establish a consistent visual language that would be recognizable, accessible, and effective in communicating with citizens.

Working closely with the municipal administration, we developed a cohesive set of visual assets that aligned with the city's values and facilitated clear communication across various platforms.

About Project

Project Information

This case study project for Aalborg Kommune involved creating a poster in both an animated digital format and a printed A3 version. The goal was to convey a warm "Merry Christmas" message to internationals living in or moving to the region, fostering a welcoming atmosphere to encourage them to continue living in Nordjylland.
