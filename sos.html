Project Details

This project focuses on capturing people's attention during their holidays at private rental houses across Denmark. The goal is to design a rack card that is both engaging and easy to read, encouraging people to explore it effortlessly while conveying essential information about the holiday properties.

Design

Project Deliverables

The design strategy focused on creating visually appealing rack cards that showcase the beauty of Denmark's holiday rentals, while providing essential information in an easily digestible format.
