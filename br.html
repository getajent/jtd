Project Details

BR Danmark is a comprehensive retail branding project that includes packaging design and visual identity. The project focused on creating a dynamic and family-friendly identity for this retail company, with an emphasis on colorful and engaging design elements catered to their target audience.

Brand Identity

The BR Danmark project focused on creating a vibrant and appealing identity that resonates with families and children while maintaining a professional appearance for the retail industry.

Using a bright and energetic color palette along with playful typography, we developed a system that embodies fun and excitement without compromising on brand recognition.

Design Strategy

The design strategy was built around these core principles that guide all visual decisions:

  • Family-Friendly: Creating a welcoming and accessible experience for families.
  • Playful: Incorporating elements that inspire joy and imagination.
  • Vibrant: Using bold colors and dynamic compositions to capture attention.
  • Consistency: Ensuring a cohesive look across all touchpoints and materials.
